About Munchi Mochi

We’re Munchi Mochi, a small, hands-on team of three based in Aotearoa, creating handmade mochi with a local twist.

Our journey started with a simple idea: take something we love, do it well, and make it our own. With backgrounds across food, design, and business, we each bring a different skill set, but what brought us together was a shared love for good food, creativity, and doing things a little differently.

Mochi stood out to us for its texture, its versatility, and how fun it is to eat. We started experimenting in our kitchens, testing ideas, and sharing them with friends and family. The feedback was better than we expected, and before long, Munchi Mochi was born.

Everything we make is small-batch and carefully crafted. From the early stages of recipe testing to hand-packing each order, we’re involved every step of the way. We’re big on quality, attention to detail, and keeping things fresh, both in ingredients and ideas.

Munchi Mochi is still a young business, but we’re proud of how far it’s come. We’re constantly learning, growing, and thinking of ways to make our product better. Whether it’s refining flavours, improving packaging, or connecting with the local community.

Thanks for stopping by and checking us out. Your support means a lot to us, and we’re excited to keep building Munchi Mochi, one bite at a time.
